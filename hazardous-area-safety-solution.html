Clean Room Equipment

FFU

This is the unit applied to super Clean Rooms requiring independent and ultra clean space for major production lines

HFU / BFU

The basic unit which collects fine contaminants from the air through the high efficiency particulate arrestance filter to purify and supply the air to the interior.

EFU

This is a forceful exhaust unit designed to eliminate static air current and eddy inside the Clean Room and quickly removes contamination source.

AIR SHOWER

By using strong wind and dust collector, It quickly eliminate and block alien substances that can be carried in with people and articles entering or leaving a clean environment

PASS BOX

This removes quickly and blocks the alien substances that can be included when moving equipment into clean environment in order to maintain the cleanness of the space.

RELIEF DAMPER

Controls the pressure difference with the neighboring chambers to maintain indoor static pressure and controls the direction of the flow by adjusting the pressure difference to prevent the backflow of the outside air.
