Company Profile

Tech Synergy is a engineering, marketing, system integration & turnkey solution provider with a clear focus on providing best value to its clientele.We offer products & engineered solutions for a wide range of applications by combining our engineering & technical resources, product knowledge & expertise in handling projects of various sizes.

We have a highly qualified and experienced team of professionals who are capable of managing technologies & projects of various sizes. We have been dealing with the leading clients in Oil & Gas, Petrochemicals and Chemicals, Power Generations, EPC, OEM and have a strong base of customers.

In order to provide high quality products and services, we have associated with World's leading organizations from USA, Europe & Asia. We have our own offices in UAE & India and associates offices in Saudi, Oman, Kuwait, Qatar, Singapore, Malaysia and a team of engineers to support our activities.
